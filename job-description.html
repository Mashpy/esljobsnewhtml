Seeking Part-Time Math Instructors

Date Posted

August 17, 2015

Location

Fremont, California

Salary

Hourly Rate

Description

Sylvan Learning of Fremont is looking for enthusiastic, dedicated and experienced tutors to teach 10 - 20 hours per week.

Language arts tutor needed for K - 12th level with a passion for teaching writing.

Math levels through high school Calculus. Any physics and chemistry expertise a plus. Teacher for STEM programs grades 1-6 also needed.

No lesson planning required. Your job is purely to teach students!

Responsibilities:

  • Deliver 3 to 1 instruction with the ability to balance instruction and provide individualized attention and feedback.
  • Provide instruction in a positive and constructive environment in order to build student confidence and success.
  • Provide meaningful feedback to Directors for effective communication with parents.

Requirements:

  • Bachelors degree in field related to subject area will be teaching. Teaching Credential required for language arts position.
  • Ability to work afternoons, evenings and weekends with availability to work a minimum of 10 hours per week.
  • Reliability and consistency of schedule in order to provide best customer service to our families Please provide resume and cover letter detailing your qualifications and goals.
